About Our Institution

Union Christian College , Aluva was established in 1921 as an ecumenical, Indian Christian initiative in the field of higher education in Kerala. A sprawling green campus spread over 40 acres, the college is located on the banks of river Periyar, about 12 kilometers away from the Cochin International Airport. Affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, the institution is known for its academic excellence and research potential. The college has 18 academic departments, with around 1900 students and 160 faculty members.
